My Teaching Method

My approach is practical, expressive, and tailored to you. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced player, I'll guide you step-by-step to play the harmonica with confidence and creativity.

You'll learn to play in a variety of styles, from Blues and Rock to Pop, Soul, World Music, Hip-Hop, and more. My goal is to help you find your unique voice on the harmonica and make music that resonates.

What You'll Achieve:

  • Play your first songs from day one.
  • Master essential techniques like bending, tongue-blocking, and vibrato.
  • Explore diverse musical styles and develop your own sound.
  • Build a solid foundation and progress to advanced skills.
  • Gain confidence to perform and improvise.

About Me

I’m Salvatore Cortone, a full-time harmonica player, multi-instrumentalist, and educator with a lifelong passion for music. From a young age, I’ve been captivated by the harmonica and its ability to convey emotion and energy across genres.

I’ve performed with bands like Hugo and the Bad Jujus, Oyun Malotira, and Magmell, and have played at festivals across Portugal and internationally. My experience spans a wide range of musical styles, and I bring that diversity to my teaching.

As an educator, I focus on helping students of all levels—from beginners to advanced players—develop their skills and find their unique sound. My lessons are designed to be engaging, practical, and fun, ensuring you make progress while enjoying the process.
